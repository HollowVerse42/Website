Our Mission

At Panterberry, our mission is to democratize artificial intelligence by making cutting-edge AI solutions accessible to businesses of all sizes. We believe that AI has the power to transform industries, solve complex problems, and create new opportunities for growth and innovation.

We are committed to developing AI solutions that are not only powerful and effective but also ethical, transparent, and responsible. We work closely with our clients to understand their unique challenges and goals, and we leverage our expertise to deliver customized AI solutions that drive real business value.

Our Journey

The story of Panterberry's growth and evolution

2015: The Beginning

Panterberry was founded by Dr. Emily Chen with a vision to make AI accessible to businesses of all sizes. Starting with a small team of 5 AI researchers, the company began developing custom AI solutions for early clients.

2017: Expansion

After successful projects in finance and healthcare, Panterberry expanded its team to 20 members and opened its first office in San Francisco. The company launched its AI Strategy & Consulting service line.

2019: Innovation

Panterberry developed its proprietary AI platform, enabling faster deployment of custom AI solutions. The company received its first industry award for innovation in AI technology.

2021: Global Growth

With a team of over 50 AI experts, Panterberry expanded globally, opening offices in London and Singapore. The company launched its AI Training & Workshops service to help clients build internal AI capabilities.

2023: Today

Today, Panterberry is a leading AI consulting and solutions provider, serving clients across industries worldwide. With a focus on ethical AI development, the company continues to push the boundaries of what's possible with artificial intelligence.
